📚 Understanding YB Pools Metrics

🔵 Virtual Price (vp)

Estimated value of 1 LP token in the pool.

vp = xcp / total_LP_supply

Virtual price grows from fees and decreases during rebalances.

💰 XCP Profit A (xcp_a)

Total fees captured by the pool over time.

This value only increases as the pool generates fees from swaps and arbitrage.

⚫ XCPX

Estimated total value of the entire pool (wBTC/crvUSD).

Represents the overall pool health and size.

⚠️ Current Pool Dynamics

What you're seeing: The black line (XCPX) should converge with the blue line (Virtual Price) over time.

Why: When pools launched, Virtual Price grew rapidly due to Refueling Protection Fee (APE tax) from quick deposits. The threshold didn't grow accordingly, causing frequent rebalances that temporarily reduce APR.

Timeline: This imbalance naturally resolves as pools accumulate more fees. Expected resolution: ~10-12 days from pool launch.

Impact of TVL growth: More liquidity = more stable prices = fewer rebalances = higher fees retention for LPs.
